Ethics

Ethics is the study of moral principles and values. It explores questions about right and wrong, good and bad, justice and fairness.

Subfields of Ethics

  • Meta-ethics: Examines the nature of moral judgments and values.
  • Normative ethics: Develops theories about what is morally right and wrong.
  • Applied ethics: Applies ethical principles to specific issues, such as bioethics or environmental ethics.
